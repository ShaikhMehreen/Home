Biryani

How has Covid impacted my life

If you told me there would be a long pandemic and a dangerous virus was going around in 2019, I would have called you crazy. Unfortunately that is exactly the situation we are all in right now. Covid harmed my life in many ways, and I really hope things are able to get back to normal soon. Firstly, I lost touch with a lot of friends during the pandemic. Despite this though, I got to know other people better and was able to strengthen our friendship. I also wasn't able to meet a lot of new people at Victoria Park. I would've loved to meet many different people and make new friends, but I was only in-person for the first quad. The only thing that kept me from being bored during quarantine was anime and mangas. I had been watching anime before the pandemic, but I was able to watch more longer shows during quarantine, as there was no school to worry about. My sleep schedule was destroyed over the quarantine, and now I can't fall asleep before 12 am. There was also a lot of news and killings during the time, and opening social media during the time felt sickening. Every other post would be about covid or killings, and I think that impacted me a lot. Overall, the pandemic was not a positive part of my live, and hopefully I am able to call it a -closed- chapter of my life soon!
