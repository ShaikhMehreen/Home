Books






I love to read books! Whether it be novels or mangas,
I'm always reading whenever I have free time.

My Favourite Books:

No Longer Human

"No Longer Human" was Osamu Dazai's last book before he committed suicide. The book was published in 1948 and follows Oba Yozo and his descent into madness. The book is able to portray the sense of lonliness and confusion Oba feels as he is unable to show his true nature to most people, and believes he is a man disqualified as a human being.

The Young Elites

the Young Elites is a Renaissance-like world where magic is looked down upon. Sixteen-year-old Adelina Amouteru survives an epidemic and is now a magic weilder. In hopes of finding other users, Adelina sets out to find a mysterious group who claim they can help "cure" Adelina of her magical curse.

Aside from novels, I love to read mangas!

Mangas are a wide variety of comic books and graphic novels produced and published in Japan. Unlike western comic books, mangas are printed in only black and white (aside from some extra pages). Manga is read from right-to-left rather than how we usually read left-to-right. Most manga authors draw each panel physically rather than digitally. I'm always suprised by how amazing the art looks in different mangas, all with unique styles. I enjoy reading mangas because there is a wide variety of genres, unique plots, and lovable characters.

My Favourite Mangas:

Here are a few of my favourite mangas I've read so far. You can click on the images to read a sample of the first chapter!

Attack on Titan

The hit series by Hajime Isayama is a very detailed and intruiging read. The story focuses on the struggle of surviving and understanding how there are always two sides to a story. The plot is very well written, along with many plot twists that are foreshadowed from the very beginning. The manga has a bitter-sweet ending bringing the story to a complete end.

The Promised Neverland

The Promised Neverland is a unique story with plot twists I didn't expect. The story follows three children Emma, Norman, and Ray who live alongside other orphans. One day they learn the orphanage is really a farm where the children are raised and given to demons to be eaten.The three work out a plan to escape with the rest of the children. The story has a bitter-sweet ending, that I enjoyed.

SpyxFamily

SpyxFamily is an action comedy manga about an international spy who recieves a rather difficult mssion. He needs to "build" a family! He ends up adopting Anya and doing a fake marriage with Yor. What he doesn't realise is that Anya is a telepath who can read minds, and Yor is an assassin! Each member goes through difficult situations in order to keep their secrets from the others.

Uzumaki

Uzumaki is one of Junji Ito's most popular mangas. The story follows Kirie and Shuichi who live in a strange town where bizarre events occur. Uzumaki is a horror manga with amazing art. It leaves you questioning life, and fearing what's in the dark. I believe the ending is satisfying, showing what the result of hardwork can be sometimes.

Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul is an amazing manga with a deep plot and beautiful art. The story follows Ken Kaneki, a teenage boy who gets into an accident and wakes up to find out he has become a ghoul. In this world, ghouls are horrifying creatures who rely on human flesh and blood as their main source of nutrition. Kaneki must decide whether he wants to survive as a ghoul, which means feeding on humans, or starving to death.

Vinland Saga

Vinland Saga is set in the times of vikings, where people invaded and fought over land and treasures. Thorfinn is a young boy who holds a grudge against a powerful viking who killed a loved one. Thorfinn wants to do a fair duel, but loses his temper quickly and never wins against his enemy. As he travels, he meets different people and grows from his experience. I enjoy this manga because it has heavy themes and explains deep morals throughout the show.

Komi Can't Communicate

Komi Can't Communicate is a wholesome romance comedy. The story follow Komi, who has a communication disorder. This means she is afraid of talking to others and is too shy to express her emotions. She meets the social Tadano and makes her first friend. Tadano make a promise to help Komi get 100 friends before high school is over. Komi tries to meet and talk to different people in order for her communication disorder to go away. Will she be able to make 100 friends before she graduates?

One-Punch Man

One-Punch Man follows the story of Saitama, a superhero who can defeat any opponent with just one punch! Even after going through many opponents, Saitama still seeks a worthy opponent who takes more than just one punch to defeat. The story is an action comedy, introducing lots of fun and entertaining characters. The art in One-Punch is amazing, some of the panels looking so detailed you wouldn't believe it's hand-drawn!
